# 🔐 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Staying safe online is essential in today’s digital world. Follow these simple steps to protect yourself from fraud and cyber threats:

---

## ✅ Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank  
- Do not scan random or unknown QR codes, especially when someone says you will receive money  
- Always double-check the person before sending money online  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages from unknown sources  
- Use strong and secure passwords for your apps  
- Keep checking your bank transactions regularly  
- Turn on SMS or email alerts for all transactions  
- If you notice any fraud, report it immediately on **cybercrime.gov.in**  
- You can also call the helpline number **1930** for quick help  

---

⚠️ *Being alert and cautious online can prevent most cyber frauds. Stay informed, stay safe!*
