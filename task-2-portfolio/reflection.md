## 💭 Reflection

In this task, I created my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Each of these platforms has a different use and helped me understand how important it is to build a digital presence as a student. GitHub is useful for uploading projects and keeping track of my coding work. LinkedIn helps in connecting with people and understanding professional opportunities. Kaggle is a good platform to practice skills and learn through real-world datasets and problems.

While working on this task, I realized that having a professional profile is not just for the future, but something I should start building from now. Over the next four years, I plan to keep updating my GitHub with projects, improve my LinkedIn profile, and stay active on Kaggle to learn new things. This will help me improve my skills and prepare for internships and future career opportunities.
